About

Business,
by design.

I'm a Business graduate who finds the most interesting questions at the edges of disciplines — where marketing meets data, where people dynamics shape strategy, and where design makes the difference between a message landing or disappearing.

My work spans marketing analytics, brand strategy and HR — not because I can't choose, but because the sharpest business thinking happens when you can move between them. I use Excel, Tableau, Jamovi and Orange to make sense of data. This site is where I think out loud about business.
