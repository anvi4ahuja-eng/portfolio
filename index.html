About

Business, through
people and data.

I'm a Business graduate with a genuine curiosity for how companies grow, compete, and connect with people. My interests sit at the intersection of marketing, analytics, branding, and strategy — and I find that the most interesting business questions live exactly there.

During my internship at Lahori Zeera, I worked across the full HR lifecycle — from recruitment and onboarding to employee engagement and operations. It taught me something I hadn't expected: understanding a business through its people is just as revealing as any spreadsheet.

I work with Excel, Tableau, Jamovi, and Orange to make sense of data — not because numbers are the destination, but because they're often the clearest path to a good decision. I'm preparing for an MBA, and I'm building this space to think out loud along the way.
